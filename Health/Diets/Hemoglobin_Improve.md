# 30-Day Vegetarian Meal Plan for Improving Hemoglobin
## Affordable Indian Foods | Iron-Rich | Seasonal | Balanced Nutrition

> **Goal**
- Improve hemoglobin naturally
- Increase iron, folate, protein, and Vitamin C intake
- Use locally available Indian foods
- Prefer seasonal vegetables and fruits
- Include one green leafy vegetable daily

# Important Tips

✔ Eat greens every day.

✔ Pair iron-rich foods with Vitamin C.

✔ Rotate pulses throughout the week.

✔ Prefer whole grains over polished rice.

✔ Use a cast-iron kadai for cooking when possible.

✔ Avoid tea or coffee around iron-rich meals.

✔ Sleep 7–8 hours daily.

Foods That Improve Iron Absorption
* Lemon juice
* Amla
* Tomato
* Guava
* Orange
* Sweet lime
Add one of these to each iron-rich meal.

Foods That Reduce Iron Absorption
Avoid close to meals:
* Tea
* Coffee
* Excess milk
* Large amounts of cheese
* Cola drinks
Wait about 1–2 hours after an iron-rich meal.

Simple Exercises
Walking
* 30–45 minutes daily
Sunlight
* 15–20 minutes in the morning
Yoga
* Surya Namaskar (5–12 rounds)
* Bhujangasana
* Vajrasana (after meals)
* Tadasana
Strength Training
* 2–3 sessions per week
* Squats
* Lunges
* Push-ups (or wall push-ups)
* Resistance-band exercises
Avoid very intense exercise if your hemoglobin is significantly low and you experience dizziness or shortness of breath.

Traditional Indian Tips
* Cook acidic foods like tomato-based curries in a cast-iron kadai when appropriate; this can increase the iron content of the meal.
* Add fresh lemon juice after cooking rather than during prolonged boiling.
* Include sprouts 2–3 times a week.
* Use a mix of millets instead of only polished white rice.
* Rotate greens instead of eating only spinach.
* Include curry leaves in chutneys, podis, or buttermilk (if dairy timing doesn't interfere with an iron-focused meal).
