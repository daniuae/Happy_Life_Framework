# Diet for Low Hemoglobin (Iron-Rich Indian Foods)

*(Sorted approximately from least expensive to most expensive. Focused
on foods commonly grown or produced in India.)*

> **Important:** Low hemoglobin can result from iron deficiency, vitamin
> B12 or folate deficiency, chronic disease, blood loss, or inherited
> conditions. Consult a healthcare professional for appropriate testing
> before taking supplements.

## General Rules

-   Include a source of **Vitamin C** with every iron-rich meal.
-   Good choices:
    -   Lemon
    -   Amla
    -   Guava
    -   Orange
    -   Tomato

### Avoid 1--2 hours before/after iron-rich meals

-   Tea
-   Coffee
-   Cola
-   Excess dairy

# Foods That Reduce Iron Absorption

-   Tea
-   Coffee
-   Excess milk
-   Cheese
-   Cola

Wait about **1--2 hours** after an iron-rich meal.

-   Yoga:
    -   Surya Namaskar
    -   Bhujangasana
    -   Vajrasana
    -   Tadasana
-   Strength training 2--3 times per week:
    -   Squats
    -   Lunges
    -   Push-ups
    -   Resistance-band exercises

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Practical Tips

-   Cook in a cast-iron kadai when appropriate.
-   Add lemon juice after cooking.
-   Eat sprouts 2--3 times weekly.
-   Prefer millets over polished rice.
-   Rotate leafy greens.
-   Include curry leaves regularly in chutneys or podis.
